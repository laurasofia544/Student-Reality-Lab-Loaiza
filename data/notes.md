# Data Notes

## Source 1: Wages
- Series: Average Hourly Earnings of All Employees, Total Private (CES0500000003)
- Source: U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics via FRED
- Retrieval date: 2026-02-27
- Units: Dollars per hour (monthly)
- Link: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/CES0500000003

## Source 2: Inflation
- Series: Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers: All Items (CPIAUCSL)
- Source: U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics via FRED
- Retrieval date: 2026-02-27
- Units: Index 1982-84=100 (monthly)
- Link: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/CPIAUCSL

## Caveats
- Wage series is an average (not student-specific).
- CPI is national; it does not represent every region equally.
- These datasets show macro trends and cannot prove individual outcomes.
